Design Details

  • Input field removed — just a "Create New Campaign" button
  • Draft campaigns use a dashed amber border to distinguish from active campaigns
  • Progress indicator shows which wizard step you're on (e.g., "Step 2 of 4 — Story & Phases")
  • "Continue" button takes you back into the wizard at the right step
  • Active campaigns link to the existing campaign view (unchanged)
  • Drafts can be deleted just like active campaigns
